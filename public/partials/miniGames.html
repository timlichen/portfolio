MiniGames - iOS Development

This project was made to show off the capabilities of the coreMotion iOS framework. This game was designed with modularity in mind so that we could integrate other mini-games modules into the platform we created. The mini-games were designed to showcases an iPhone's gyroscope, accelerometer, and device attitude/position.

Personal Contributions to project:
  • coreMotion research and integration
  • mini-game developement
Colleagues:

Raymond Luu and Dylan Sharkey

Application Development and Process

During our development process we decided that the best way to work on this project was to develop a framework that would incorporate the governing rules of the application for example, the duration of each mini game, transitioning randomly to the next game, and how the app would look.

I worked on learning more about the coreMotion framework, its capabilities, game concepts, how to turn the sensors on and off, how to get the device to vibrate, and how to interpret the data returned from the sensors.
